CSFP Requirement — Racial & Ethnic Data Collection

Each local agency and/or each sub-site shall collect the number of participants receiving food packages by racial/ethnic category during the month of April each year, unless otherwise specified by CDSS. Self-identification or self-reporting of this information is the preferred method. The FNS-191 report must be submitted each year to CDSS.
